Accessibility Statement

This statement was last updated on: [19 July 2026 09:52PM CT]

We at Casmir Contracting Company | LLC are committed to making our website accessible to all individuals, including those with disabilities. Our goal is to provide an inclusive, user-friendly experience for everyone, regardless of ability, technology, or circumstance. We continuously work to improve the usability of our website and align our content with recognized accessibility standards.

What web accessibility is

An accessible website allows visitors with disabilities to browse and interact with the site with the same or a similar level of ease and enjoyment as other users. This can be achieved through the capabilities of the platform on which the site is built, as well as through compatibility with assistive technologies such as screen readers, keyboard navigation, and other accessibility tools.

Accessibility adjustments on this site

We have adapted this site in accordance with WCAG 2.1 guidelines and strive to maintain a level of AA accessibility compliance. Our efforts to support accessibility include:

  • Maintaining clear and consistent navigation throughout the website
  • Structuring content with clear headings and logical organization
  • Enhancing readability and simplicity of content
  • Using color combinations that meet recommended contrast standards
  • Adding alternative text to images where applicable
  • Supporting keyboard navigation and screen reader compatibility where possible
  • Reducing unnecessary motion or visual distractions
  • Ensuring that media and content are presented in an accessible format when feasible

Declaration of Partial Compliance Due to Third-Party Content

Certain areas of this website may rely on third-party services or platforms, including website infrastructure, third-party services, and other embedded tools. While we strive to ensure full accessibility across all pages, some elements may not be fully within our control. As a result, we declare partial compliance with accessibility standards for portions of the site that depend on third-party content or functionality.

Ongoing Commitment

Accessibility is an ongoing effort. We regularly review our website to identify and address potential accessibility barriers. While we aim for full compliance, some content may not yet be fully optimized due to updates in progress, technological limitations, or evolving accessibility standards.

Requests, Issues, and Suggestions

If you experience any difficulty accessing content on our website, or if you have suggestions on how we can improve accessibility, we encourage you to contact us. Your feedback is important and helps us continue to improve.
